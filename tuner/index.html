What is it?

It's a Subaru! USDM spec 2.0L Impreza Sport CVT in Premium trim. It works. It is primaly choosen by people who value safety, utility and AWD.

  • Spacious 5 door compact hatchback (GP chassis) (22.5/52.4 cubic feet)
  • Scrappy NA 2.0L 4-cylinder (FB20B Boxer engine) (148hp/145tq)
  • Smooth Lineartronic CVT (TR580 transmission) (Automaic/6sp manual-mode)
  • Surefooted symmetrical AWD (Active Torque Split drivetrain) (VDC)

Drivetrain

  • All-Wheel-Drive
  • Active Torque Split - electronically controlled multi-plate transfer clutch
    • 60% F / 40% R : default
    • 50% F / 50% R : on-demand
  • Vehicle Dynamics Control (VDC)

The FB20 Engine

  • Naturally Apsirated
  • 2.0L Flat-4 cylinder
  • DOHC w/ AVCS
  • 148hp (110 kw) @6200rpm
  • 145 ft/lbs (197 N-m) @4200rpm
  • 10.5:1 compression ratio
  • 0w-20w oil

TR580 CVT

  • CVT w/ torque converter
  • 6 speed manual shift mode
  • Max engine torque 235 N-m
  • Stall torque ratio 2.07
  • Max torque input to CVT = 235 x 2.07 = 486 N-m

Wheels, Tires, Suspension and Brakes

  • 17x7 alloy sport wheels 5x100 +48 offset
  • Yokohama AVID S34D 205/50/R17
  • 5.9" ground clearance
  • Front:
    • MacStrut
    • 22mm sway bar
    • 10.9-in. ventilated discs
  • Rear:
    • Double wishbone
    • 16mm sway bar
    • 10.8-in. solid discs
