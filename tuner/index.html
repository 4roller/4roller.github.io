Subaru Impreza Sport 2.0i CVT - 2016

  • 5 door compact hatchback - GP chassis
  • NA 2.0L 4-cylinder - FB20B Boxer engine
  • Lineartronic CVT - TR580 transmission
  • Symmetrical AWD - Active Torque Split drivetrain

The FB20 Engine

  • Naturally Apsirated
  • 2.0L Flat-4 cylinder
  • DOHC w/ AVCS
  • 148hp (110 kw) @6200rpm
  • 145 ft/lbs (197 N-m) @4200rpm
  • 10.5:1 compression ratio
  • 0w-20w oil

TR580 CVT

  • CVT w/ torque converter
  • 6 speed manual shift mode
  • Max engine torque 235 N-m
  • Stall torque ratio 2.07
  • Max torque input to CVT = 235 x 2.07 = 486 N-m

Drivetrain

  • All-Wheel-Drive
  • Active Torque Split - electronically controlled multi-plate transfer clutch
    • 60% F / 40% R : default
    • 50% F / 50% R : on-demand
  • Vehicle Dynamics Control (VDC)

Wheels, Suspension, Brakes

  • Yokohama AVID S34D 205/50/R17
  • 17x7 alloy sport wheels
  • Front:
    • MacStrut
    • 22mm sway bar
    • 10.9-in. ventilated discs
  • Rear:
    • Double wishbone
    • 16mm sway bar
    • 10.8-in. solid discs
