What is this?

This is the a corner weight calculator

You can calculate the Front vs Rear weight bias, and also the Left vs Right weight bias.

How to use: Place a scale under one wheel of your vehicle, support the rest of the wheels with a platform the same height of your scale. Enter the weights in the input boxes for:

  • Front Left
  • Front Right
  • Rear Left
  • Rear Right

Seeing a % error? If you are seeing a % error, it is because your measured corner weights do not add up to the entered total weight.
