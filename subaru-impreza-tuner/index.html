What is it?

It's a Subaru
More specifically it's a USDM 2016 Subaru Impreza Sport Hatchback 2.0i CVT in Premium trim. These are the last Impreza's to be built in Gunma, Japan. I took delivery in Los Angeles, California.

The contents of these pages are about tuning an economy car, specifically the Subaru Impreza (2012-2016), and XV Crosstrek (2013-2017). Here you will find information on the GP chassis, FB20 engine, TR580 CVT, Subaru suspension, drivetrain and other vehicular topics. Please enjoy!
