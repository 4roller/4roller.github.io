What is it?

It's a Subaru!
More specifically it's a USDM 2016 Subaru Impreza Sport Hatchback 2.0i CVT in Premium trim. These are the last Imprezas to be built in Gunma, Japan. I took delivery in Los Angeles, California.

Lineage

The GP7 hatchback chassis comes from a long line of Subaru wagons starting namely of the iconic first generation GF Outback Sport Wagon. The GG was the second generation 5 door wagon. Its direct predecessor, the 3rd generation Impreza GH(narrow) / GR (wide) hatchback shares a similar unibody design and has competed in rally and motorsports . You might say that the 4th generation GP(7) chassis also shares this motorsports pedigree. The same GP chassis is used in both the Impreza hatchback and the XV Crosstrek. The GT chassis code is the 5th generation 5-door wagon/hatchback. For all intents and purposes, the tuning and modifications contained in these pages are on the GP chassis but may also relate to other subaru models past and present.
