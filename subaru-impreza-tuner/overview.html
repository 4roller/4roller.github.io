2016 Subaru Impreza Sport CVT - Overview

This is a sport compact hatchback with interior room to spare

Features

  • Spacious 5 door compact hatchback (GP chassis)
  • Scrappy NA 2.0L 4-cylinder (FB20B Boxer engine)
  • Smooth Lineartronic CVT (TR580 transmission)
  • Surefooted symmetrical AWD (Active Torque Split)

Stock Specifications:

  • Coefficient of drag (Cd): 0.32
  • Wheelbase: 104.1"
  • Weight: 3,109 lbs
  • Ride Height: 5.9"
  • Est. Top Speed: 120 mph
  • 17"x7" wheels 205/50/R17 tyres
  • Length: 174"
  • Width: 68.5"
  • Height: 59.4"
  • Track (f/r): 59.4"/59.6"

Lineage

The GP7 hatchback chassis comes from a long line of Subaru wagons starting namely of the iconic first generation GF Outback Sport Wagon. The GG was the second generation 5 door wagon. Its direct predecessor, the 3rd generation Impreza GH(narrow) / GR (wide) hatchback shares a similar uni-body design and has competed in rally and other racing events . You might say that the 4th generation GP(7) chassis also shares this motor sports pedigree. The same GP chassis is used in both the Impreza hatchback and the XV Crosstrek. The GT chassis code is the 5th generation 5-door wagon/hatchback. For all intents and purposes, the tuning and modifications contained in these pages are on the GP chassis but may also relate to other subaru models past and present.
