Subaru FB20B Engine - BOXER

The FB20B was designed as an economy engine, it has a peak horsepower and torque that are typical of similar engines of the same displacement. Being natually aspirated and utilizing port injection it is meant to serve as a sort of workhorse, reliable and economical. It has horizontally apposed pistons which helps lower the center of gravity when placed in the engine bay. The name of this style of engine is the boxer.

Engine Specifications

  • Naturally Apsirated - port injection
  • 145 ft/lbs (197 N-m) @4200rpm
  • 148hp (110 kw) @6200rpm
  • 10.5:1 compression ratio
  • 2.0L Flat-4 cylinder
  • DOHC w/ AVCS
  • 0w-20w oil
